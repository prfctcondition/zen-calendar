# Privacy Policy for Zen Calendar

*Last updated: June 26, 2026*

## Data Collection

Zen Calendar accesses your Google Calendar data solely to display your events within the application. The app reads calendar data with **read-only** permission — it never creates, modifies, or deletes any events.

## Data Storage

- **Authentication tokens** are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server other than Google's OAuth endpoints.
- **No data** is sent to any third-party server, analytics service, or tracking service.
- Zen Calendar has no backend server — it runs entirely on your computer.

## Data Sharing

Zen Calendar does not collect, share, or sell any personal data. All data remains on your local machine.

## Third-Party Services

Zen Calendar uses the Google Calendar API. By authenticating, you agree to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Zen Calendar only uses the `calendar.readonly` OAuth scope.
